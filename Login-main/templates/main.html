About Us

The Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences has a well equipped lab facility for students of the five-year Integrated M.Sc SS/TCS/DS, M.Sc Applied Mathematics and B.Sc Computer Systems and Design. Every student will be provided with login credentials for use throughout the course time. Every lab is equipped with state of the art facilities. There are also domain specific labs which make use of technologies in those domains in innovative ways. The lab has more than 400 computers, which are connected to the centralized servers. The lab has a domain server, database server, hadoop server, Linux server and a GPU server. All the latest version of tools and technologies are available to meet the industrial requirements. The labs shall stay open from 8:30 am to 10 pm during work days, till 5 pm on Saturdays and as required during Sundays and holidays.

OUR VISION

The fundamental objective of the department is to develop quality professionals by providing concept oriented subject knowledge through high quality teaching supplemented with practical training. Apart from specialized knowledge and skills, the programmes conducted by the Department aim to develop the personality of students by inculcating values of honesty, sincerity, team spirit and work culture.
